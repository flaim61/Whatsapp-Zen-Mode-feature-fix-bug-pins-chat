INSTALL EXTENSION
0. Unpack extension.zip into a folder.
1. In chrome Goto chrome://extensions/.
2. In top right corner toggle 'developer mode'.
3. In top left corner press  'load unpackaged extension'.
4. Point to the folder of where you've unpacked the archive.
5. BAM! you've got it installed. It's icon will appear in top right corner, in the extensions bar.

6. You can modify RELEASE-NOTES.yaml as needed. The format used is YAML - a better alternative to JSON.
